ABOUT


As a self-taught artist, my journey has been a rewarding exploration of various mediums, techniques, and styles. This self-directed approach has allowed me to develop a unique artistic voice, driven by constant discovery and experimentation. The freedom to pursue my interests without external pressure has led to a diverse portfolio that reflects my eclectic tastes.


My background in interior design has sharpened my eye for detail and composition, and I’m currently expanding my skills in UI/UX design at Conestoga College. Here, I’m mastering app design, focusing on prototypes, wireframes, and visual interaction design. These experiences together give me a unique, holistic perspective on creative challenges.
